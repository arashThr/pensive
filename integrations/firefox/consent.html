Welcome!

Pensive Chrome Extension is the best way to save articles to Pensive and build your searchable memory of the web.

Information We Collect

In order to save articles to Pensive, we collect certain information as part of this Chrome Extension:

  • URLs of articles you save
  • HTML content of articles you save (for better content extraction and searchability)
  • Page metadata (title, description, publication date)

Pensive only collects information when you save an article or take an action.

Your Consent

Before continuing, we need your consent to collect data in the manner described above.

Enhanced Capture (Recommended): Captures HTML content for better searchability and content preservation.
Server-Side Only: Only sends the URL to our servers for processing. May not work on all sites.
