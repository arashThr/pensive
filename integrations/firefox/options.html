How to use the extension:

1. Connect your account: Click "Connect to Pensive" and sign in with your account credentials.

2. Save pages instantly: Click the Pensive icon in your browser toolbar to save the current page to your library.

3. Manage bookmarks: The popup shows if a page is already saved and lets you remove it with one click.

4. Search and access: Use the search link to quickly find and access your saved content.
