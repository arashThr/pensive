Content Extraction Methods Explained

🔒 Server-Side Extraction

How it works: Extension sends only the page URL to Pensive servers. Servers then fetch and process the page content.

Data shared: Only the URL - no page content leaves your browser

Quality: Excellent - Uses advanced server-side processing

Best for: Public articles, news sites, blog posts

📄 Smart Article Extraction (Recommended)

How it works: Browser uses Mozilla Readability to extract clean article content locally, then sends only the main text to Pensive.

Data shared: Clean article content - ads and navigation removed

Quality: Excellent - Focuses on main content, removes noise

Best for: Most articles, blog posts, news stories

⚠️ Full Page Extraction

How it works: Browser captures entire page content, cleans it locally, then sends processed HTML to Pensive.

Data shared: Complete page content after cleaning

Quality: Most complete - Captures everything, including content that article detection might miss

Best for: Reddit posts, forums, complex layouts where article detection fails

⚠️ Your responsibility: Be mindful of what content you're sharing. Don't use for pages with personal information, emails, or private data you don't want stored.
